Your complete novel writing platform

Get Started Quickly

Creating an account is quick and easy. All that we need is your email address and a password. Our setup wizard will help you get your first novel project set up and ready in just a few easy steps.

Multiple Projects

NovelMate allows you to create and manage up to 10 projects all from one unified application. Each project supports its own plot, set of characters, and privacy settings.

Character Development

To test without limits we can form teams to try new metas and prove (or disprove) strategies. A five summoner group won't draw flames in champion select but may leave the opposition awestruck in post-game.

Share Your Novel

Want to share your new novel with your friends? Each project come with its own privacy settings that allow you to control who can, and who can't, see your project. Keep your project to yourself, show it to your friends, or publish it to the whole world!

Frequently asked questions

Can I access my projects from multiple computers?

Absolutely! All of your projects and data will be stored on our data servers. This means that you will be able to access your projects anywhere where you have an internet connection


Is NovelMate free?

Creating and managing novels is completely free with a NovelMate account. Some features are locked unless you use a professional account. You can see details, as well as pricing, on our accounts page
